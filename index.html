About Me

Hello! I am a biracial actress, native Japanese speaker, musician, dancer, and scientist. I graduated with a B.S. in neuroscience from Tufts University - what?! I know, I know, here's the short version: Growing up, my studies lead me to Japan, France, India, Argentina, and South Africa. By living with local families in these countries I had the privilage of seeing the world through each unique community's lens - to see how food customs, healthcare, and culture are all interwoven. Through these experiences, I was introduced to the power of storytelling and its significance in every human's life and the impact it can have on people. I thus realized I had to pursue my dream of acting! My goal is to weave together my passion for global health and science with this creative art of storytelling. Wish me luck!
